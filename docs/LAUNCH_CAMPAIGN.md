# TheTrickest Launch Campaign Strategy

> Created: March 5, 2026 | Status: Planning

## TL;DR

- **Best launch window:** Go Skateboarding Day (June 21) or before summer
- **Primary channel:** TikTok (algorithm surfaces content regardless of followers)
- **Secondary:** Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Reddit (r/skateboarding 400K+)
- **Key tactic:** Recruit 20-50 micro-influencer ambassadors (status, not cash)
- **Cold-start fix:** Get 50-100 quality submissions before public launch via beta testers
- **$0 is viable** with consistent organic content + ambassador activation

---

## 1. Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-4)

### Waitlist & Beta
- Landing page with email capture + countdown timer
- "First 100 skaters get Founding Member badge" incentive
- Recruit 20-30 beta testers from Reddit/Discord
- Collect video testimonials from beta users

### Content Series
- "10 Levels of TheTrickest" -- one level reveal per day for 10 days
- Behind-the-scenes of the platform on Instagram Stories
- "Can you land this?" challenge clips on TikTok (4-5x/week)

### Ambassador Recruitment
- DM 50 skaters with 5K-50K followers who post trick content
- Offer: "Founding Judge/Ambassador status + verified badge + early access"
- No cash needed -- status and access are the currency
- Target: US, Mexico, Spain, Latin America, Australia
- **Tiers:**
  - Founding Judges (5-10): Respected skaters who evaluate submissions
  - Founding Skaters (20-50): Content creators who populate the platform
  - Street Team (unlimited): Anyone sharing referral link

### Social Setup
- TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Reddit accounts
- Discord server for beta testers and ambassadors
- Press kit: one-pager, screenshots, platform description

## 2. Launch Day

### Morning
- Product Hunt launch (Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT)
- Waitlist email blast
- All ambassadors post simultaneously (coordinated via Discord)

### Midday
- Engage every Product Hunt comment
- Reddit posts in r/skateboarding, r/NewSkaters (genuine, not spammy)
- Share real-time signup milestones on Stories

### Evening
- Post first user submission highlights
- Instagram/TikTok Live with founders + a judge
- Activate Launch Day special challenge (24-hour exclusive)

### Driving Signups
- Founding Member Badge: First 500 signups
- Launch Day Challenge: 24-hour exclusive
- Referral: "Invite 3 friends, unlock early access to Level 10"

## 4. Retention & Engagement

### Gamification
- **Streaks:** Weekly submission streak badges
- **Badges:** Level completion, milestones (10/50/100 submissions), role badges
- **Leaderboards:** Global, country/region, weekly reset, teams, "Rising Stars"
- **Progressive unlock:** Higher levels unlock as lower ones are completed

### Monthly Rhythm

| Week | Theme |
|------|-------|
| 1 | New Monthly Challenge launch + demo video |
| 2 | Leaderboard updates, "Can anyone beat [user]?" |
| 3 | Community spotlight (skater profiles, team features) |
| 4 | Results, stats recap, next month teaser |

### Notifications
- "Your submission has been judged!" (highest open rate)
- "[Friend] just submitted -- can you beat their score?"
- "You're 1 submission away from your weekly streak badge!"
- Weekly digest email: top submissions, leaderboard changes

## 6. Competitive Landscape

| Platform | Strengths | Weakness | Lesson |
|----------|-----------|----------|--------|
| SkateYou | 15K+ spots, prizes | Outdated UI, video bugs | Polish UX before launch |
| Loke | 90K users, brand challenges | Technical crashes | Brand sponsors = growth flywheel |
| Pulled | Async game of SKATE | Limited scope | Async model works globally |
| EA Skate. | 15M players in 3 weeks | AAA game, different category | Seasonal content model works |

### TheTrickest Differentiators
1. **Bilingual (EN/ES)** -- No competitor offers this. Latin America is underserved
2. **Real judging (0-100 scores)** -- Not just community votes
3. **10 progressive levels** -- Clear identity: "I'm a Level 6 skater"
4. **Web-based** -- No app download needed, share a link
5. **Spots + Challenges** -- Two reasons to open the platform daily
